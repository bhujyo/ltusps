Regular Events

Where: Virtual
When: Tuesdays at 2 PM EDT/EST

At our regular SPS Meetings, we discuss upcoming events, fundraisng, recruitment, our participation in the APS-IDEA, and other topics of the day. Like all other SPS events, meetings are open to all!

Where: Virtual
When: Every other Tuesday from 12 PM to 2 PM EST/EDT

At our Study and Tutoring Sessions, students can come to study as a group, and recieve tutoring if necessary, particularly in math and physics.

Where: Virtual
When: Every Saturday at 4 PM EDT/EST

At game night, we play video games online as a group! We make sure to play games that are easily accessible even for those who do not have them!
