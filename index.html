Dr. Maria Zavodszky to give Guest Lecture For LTU SPS.

When: 22 Feb 2021, 6 PM EST

Dr. Maria Zavodszky, a senior scientist at Biogen, will be giving a virtual talk to LTU SPS on February 22nd. Her work includes analyzing next-generation sequencing data, interpreting mutational and differential expression information in pathway and disease context, gene set enrichment analysis, tissue heterogeneity analysis, and cell type deconvolution using gene expression data. During her career, she has worked in multiple biomedical areas that employ methods and techniques deeply rooted in the physical sciences. She will briefly describe several of these methods and techniques, including flow cytometry and cell sorting, structurebased drug design, pharmacokinetic modeling for imaging agent development and sequencing techniques used for gene expression analysis.
